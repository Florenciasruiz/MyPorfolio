¡Welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Florencia Ruiz. I'm a Lawyer and UX/UI designer based in Córdoba, Argentina. I seek to create light, aesthetic and minimalistic websites that solve user problems through effective and proven solutions.

Although my beginnings were in law, my path envolved towards technology and social networks. Currently I focus on Web3, creating content and facilitating digital connections. As a constant learner, I seek to explore and understand the nuances of digital evolution. In every project, I seek a collaborative environment, solving challenges with critical and flexible thinking. My focus is continuous innovation, co-creating digital experiences that transcend expectations. I am 27 years old and I graduated as a lawyer at Blas Pascal University. In 2022 I started as a web developer at Coderhouse and since then I have improved my UX/UI skills so far.
